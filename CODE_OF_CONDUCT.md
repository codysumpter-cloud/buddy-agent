# Code of Conduct

Buddy-Agent welcomes practical, respectful collaboration.

## Expected behavior

- Be direct, kind, and constructive.
- Assume good intent while still reviewing safety claims carefully.
- Keep feedback focused on code, docs, tests, architecture, and user impact.
- Respect privacy. Do not ask contributors to reveal secrets, private paths, browser sessions, or account details.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, threats, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing another person's private information.
- Encouraging unsafe automation, credential exposure, financial execution, or account abuse.
- Using issues or PRs to solicit secrets or private operational data.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or remove content and may restrict participation when behavior harms project safety or collaboration.
